Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

Uplist old was started with an idea of providing a medium to the common people where they would come and sell their old items which were of no use to them now.
This thought struck us when we were shifting from one place to another and had to sell our old products.
This seems as a very simple job to do but in actual case this is not that easy either we had to spend over a month looking for buyers and still couldn't find enough to sell everything to the right people in the desired amount in which we wanted to sell. Everyone, to whom we met said that they want to buy old products but it is really tough to find sellers.
Then this idea came, that we build a platform where both, buyers and sellers come together and the sellers can sell the products at the desired amount in which they want to sell and the buyers can buy it according to their budget and requirements.
